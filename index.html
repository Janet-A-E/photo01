About Janet

Hello! I'm Janet, a wedding photographer from NR PURA, currently pursuing my BCA at yenepoya university manglore.From past 2 years i've been lucky enough to travel around capturing one of the most beautiful things in life -love stories
with over 8 years of experience capturing love stories across the globe. My approach combines artistic vision with documentary storytelling to create timeless images that reflect the unique essence of each couple.

I believe that every love story is unique and deserves to be told with authenticity and elegance. My style is romantic yet natural, focusing on genuine emotions and candid moments that truly represent your special day.

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me exploring new destinations, spending time with my family, or planning the next adventure. I'm honored to be part of your journey and look forward to creating beautiful memories together.
